The Process

How It Works

Every name is verified by cultural experts before going live.

🎙️

Step 01

Native Recording

Audio captured from verified native speakers across Africa, in their home regions.

🔬

Step 02

Cultural Review

Meaning, origin and syllabic structure reviewed by cultural experts before publishing.

📚

Step 03

Context Added

Each name enriched with etymology, regional context and phonetic guidance.

🌍

Step 04

Published Live

Approved names go live instantly, streamed from our global cloud infrastructure.
